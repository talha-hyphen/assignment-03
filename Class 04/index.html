Interaction of organisms in Ecosystem

Interaction may be interspecific and intraspecific.Interspecific interaction between organisms of two different specices while intraspecific interaction occur between organisms of same specie.

Competition

There is a competition among organisms for resources like food,selter,water,light etc.Intraspecific competition is more harder than interspecfic.It maintains weight balance on earth.

Predation

This interacon is interspecfic,in which a organism attacks on other,kill and eat it called a Predator While other which is being attacked,killed and eaten by other called prey.e.g,Hawk attack on snake and eat it, now hawk called predator and snake called prey Similarly,Lion attack on deer,lion called predator and deer called prey.

Some plants like carnivore plants also show prey-predator relationship.For example plant,sundew,venus flytrap etc which attacks on small insects to fulfil their nitrogen deficiency.They are present in nitrogen deficient areas.They capture insects by producing sweet nector which attracts them.
